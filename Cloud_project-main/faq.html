Q: Why should I use this app?
A: Our app offers a simple, fast, and real-time cab booking experience. With just a few taps, you can select a location on the map and track your cab until it arrives. Built using secure cloud services, it's reliable and user-friendly.
Q: How do I get started?
A: Begin by registering on the home page. Once registered, log in to access the map and request a cab to your pinned location.
Q: How do I request a cab?
A: After logging in, go to the map, pin your desired pickup location, and click on the “Request Cab” button. A car icon will appear and move toward your location.
Q: How do I know my cab has arrived?
A: Once the car icon reaches your pinned location on the map, a “Car Reached” message will be displayed to notify you.
Q: What technology powers this app?
A: The app is built using cloud technologies like AWS to ensure smooth functionality, secure login, and real-time cab tracking.
